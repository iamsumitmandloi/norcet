## 2025 — NORCET 9

### Pharmacology

**Q1. Which vitamin is given if the patient has been prescribed Isoniazid?**

| Option | Answer |

## 2020 — AIIMS Nagpur Nursing Officer

### Pediatrics

**Q5. An order for prednisolone reads 10 mg four times a day. The dose for the child is 2 mg/kg/day. How many pounds does the child weigh?**

| Option | Answer |
|--------|--------|
| a) | 36 pounds |
| b) | 40 pounds |
| c) | 46 pounds |
| **d)** | **44 pounds ✓** |

> **Explanation:** Total daily dose = 10 mg × 4 = 40 mg. Weight = 40 ÷ 2 = 20 kg. 20 kg × 2.2 = 44 pounds.
